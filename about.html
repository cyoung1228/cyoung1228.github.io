About

Hello, my name is Christian Young. I am a fourth year Computer Engineering Student at the University of Florida. Through the streamlining of user experiences on personal electronic devices, I aspire to create an experience that gives the user control of what they want to control and takes care of what they don't for them. I have experience as an intern at Spincore Technologies utilizing embeded electronics systems and helping to create new magnetic resonance, mass spectrometry, spintronics, ultrasound, MRI, and related resonance and test technologies at the ground level. I am proficient working with C++, Java, Swift, VHDL, HTML, and CSS. I am also an avid surfer and golfer, spending much of my free time in the surf or on the links.
